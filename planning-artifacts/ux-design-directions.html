Design Directions for EU Solicit

Direction 1: The "Vercel" Approach

High contrast borders, monochrome styling with a single primary accent, extremely data-dense.

Direction 2: The "Notion" Approach

Frameless layout, focus on typography and whitespace, subtle dividers.

Direction 3: The "Linear" Approach

Dark mode optimized (or high-contrast light mode), dense tables, keyboard-first styling.
